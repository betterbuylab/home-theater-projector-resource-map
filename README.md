## Buyer-fit questions

1. Can you reliably darken the room for most viewing sessions?
2. Is ceiling mount feasible, or must the projector sit on a shelf?
3. Do you need ultra-short-throw placement near the wall?
4. Is gaming latency on a projector as important as on a TV?
5. Will ambient light during daytime viewing limit projector appeal?
6. Are you comparing a large TV against projector screen cost and setup friction?
7. Do you need lens shift or zoom for awkward room geometry?
8. Is fan noise acceptable in a small media room?
