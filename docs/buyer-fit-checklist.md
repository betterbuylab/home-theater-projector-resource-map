# Buyer-fit checklist

- [ ] Can you reliably darken the room for most viewing sessions?
- [ ] Is ceiling mount feasible, or must the projector sit on a shelf?
- [ ] Do you need ultra-short-throw placement near the wall?
- [ ] Is gaming latency on a projector as important as on a TV?
- [ ] Will ambient light during daytime viewing limit projector appeal?
- [ ] Are you comparing a large TV against projector screen cost and setup friction?
- [ ] Do you need lens shift or zoom for awkward room geometry?
- [ ] Is fan noise acceptable in a small media room?
